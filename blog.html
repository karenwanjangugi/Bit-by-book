    PATRICIA AS A PROTAGONISTS:Born A Crime Book Review

    Written by Karen Ngugi

    "When it was time to pick my name, she chose Trevor, a name with no meaning whatsoever in South Africa, no precedent in my family.
    It's not even a Biblical name. It's just a name. My mother wanted her child beholden to no fate. She wanted me to be free to go anywhere, do anything, be anyone."

    Patricia Nombuyiselo Noah, is a fearless, devout and nonetheless independent woman who single-handedly mothered Trevor Noah eversince he was a small kid. Patricia was the middle child to Tempererance and frances Noah, who despite being her parents, never looked out for her as they sent her to their Xhosa homeland as a teenager where she worked on the family farm and starved. This formed her resilient character and her survival skills which would help her navigate her adult life as the mother to Trevor.

    Patricia was devoted to showing Trevor that every opportunity is accessible to anyone, especially someone from their class and race.She managed to protect Trevor by hiding him inside and at times pretending that she is her maid to avoid being seen together in public, this was majorly because she loved him and would not afford to lose him to the law.
